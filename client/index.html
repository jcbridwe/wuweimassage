About Wu Wei

Jeanie Bridwell: Massage Therapist

"I believe that a good therapeutic massage is intuitive and should work with your body. The body has a wisdom of its own."

A graduate of the Medical Arts School in Raleigh NC, Jeanie is a motivated and personable therapeutic professional with a wide range of experience. She is licensed in the state of NC and VA. and continues to enhance her education in the areas of physical therapy, kinesiology, and energy work.

Her philosophy: "I believe that you can give good, deep massage work that can produce effective results without being so aggressive. Once the body is relaxed, that is when it is most receptive to deep work. The body has it's own wisdom, and loves our attention!"

Wu Wei refers to effortless action, an idea from Taoism that means effortlessly moving with the flow of life. The energy of life moves through us, and is all things; this is the philosophy inherent in my massage practice.

Services

  • Swedish - A relaxing and rejuvenating massage, if you don't get massage often, or if you are new to massage, this is a good option.
  • Deep Tissue - Deep, slow, specific. Deep Tissue gets to the deeper muscle layers and can still be very relaxing as it moves through the fascia and deeper layers of muscles.
  • Sports - Specifically focuses on massaging muscles in training, before, during, or after a sports event.
  • Trigger Point - A hyper-irritable spot within a taut band of muscle. Sometimes thought of as knots, in laymen's terms, a 'crick' in the neck is a good example, but trigger points can be anywhere. This work can be more challenging for the client but the results can bring sweet relief
